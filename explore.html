Explore Sioux Falls

Welcome to South Dakota's largest city. From the iconic falls to rich Native American heritage — here is everything worth knowing about Sioux Falls.

Start Here

Must-See Attractions

💧

Falls Park

The Big Sioux River cascades over ancient pink quartzite rock formations right in the heart of the city. The most visited attraction in South Dakota after Mount Rushmore.

✓ Free — Open Daily
🏛️

Washington Pavilion

Arts, sciences and performing arts under one roof. The Kirby Science Discovery Center, art galleries and the Husby Performing Arts Center all in one building.

Admission Required
🦅

Native American Heritage

South Dakota is home to nine Native American tribes. Discover the culture, art, history and traditions that have shaped this land for thousands of years.

✓ Many Free
Get Outside

Outdoor Activities

🚴 Bike the Big Sioux Trail

Over 30 miles of paved trail running along the Big Sioux River. Perfect for biking, running or a casual walk through beautiful scenery.

🌲 Good Earth State Park

A historic site with over 1,200 acres of natural beauty. Hiking trails, wildlife viewing and cultural history all in one park south of Sioux Falls.

🦌 Outdoor Campus

Free hands-on programs teaching fishing, archery, wildlife ID and more. Great for families and kids. Free admission, free programming.

⛷️ Great Bear Recreation Park

Skiing and snowboarding in winter, disc golf and outdoor fun in summer. The only ski area in eastern South Dakota right inside city limits.
